Job/Internships:

SCA: The SCA provides an array of environmental programs designed for a range of ages and a variety of conservation needs. While they also have alot of paid interships for high school and college students throughout the nation.

UW Undergrad Internship: The Climate Impacts Group is seeking an intern to support several interrelated projects as part of the Northwest Climate Resilience Collaborative. The intern will join EarthLab’s Summer Internship Program, a robust, cohort-based internship program for UW undergraduate students. Interns will recieve a stripend at the end of the summer internship.

City of Seattle Green Jobs: The City of Seattle is investing $2.24 million to recruit, train, and place workers from economically distressed communities into competitive, well-paying construction and clean energy jobs. They expected to hire even more people in future.
